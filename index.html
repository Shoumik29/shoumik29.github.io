Research & Projects

A Transformer-Based Segmenter Optimized for Small Datasets

Feb 2023 - Oct 2024

THESIS RESEARCH

Transformer-based segmentation architecture was proposed and optimized for retinal blood vessel segmentation under limited training data conditions. The study investigates challenges such as overfitting, computational complexity, and weak locality inductive bias in Transformer-based models for medical image segmentation. Architectural modifications were introduced to improve feature learning and data efficiency, followed by extensive evaluation on retinal fundus image datasets.
